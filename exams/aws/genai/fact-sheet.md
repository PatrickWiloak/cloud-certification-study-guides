## ðŸ“‹ Domain Breakdown

### Domain 1: Fundamentals of AI and ML (20%)
**Task Statements:**
- Explain basic AI concepts and terminology
- Identify business use cases for AI/ML
- Describe the ML development lifecycle
- Distinguish between different types of ML models

**Key Topics:**
- Supervised vs Unsupervised vs Reinforcement Learning
- Classification, Regression, Clustering
- Training, Validation, Testing datasets
- Overfitting, Underfitting, Bias-Variance tradeoff
- Model evaluation metrics

### Domain 2: Fundamentals of Generative AI (24%)
**Task Statements:**
- Explain generative AI concepts and capabilities
- Describe foundation models and their applications
- Identify prompt engineering techniques
- Understand Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)

**Key Topics:**
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Diffusion models for image generation
- Prompt engineering best practices
- Fine-tuning vs. In-context learning
- RAG architecture and implementation
- Vector databases and embeddings

### Domain 3: Applications of Foundation Models (28%)
**Task Statements:**
- Select appropriate foundation models for use cases
- Understand Amazon Bedrock capabilities
- Describe model customization options
- Plan inference and deployment strategies

**Key Topics:**
- Amazon Bedrock model providers (Anthropic, AI21, Cohere, etc.)
- Model selection criteria
- Inference parameters and optimization
- Custom model training and fine-tuning
- Cost optimization for AI workloads

### Domain 4: Guidelines for Responsible AI (14%)
**Task Statements:**
- Identify AI bias and fairness considerations
- Understand transparency and explainability requirements
- Recognize ethical implications of AI systems
- Apply responsible AI frameworks

**Key Topics:**
- Algorithmic bias detection and mitigation
- Model interpretability and explainability
- Fairness metrics and evaluation
- Human oversight and control
- AI ethics frameworks

### Domain 5: Security, Compliance, and Governance (14%)
**Task Statements:**
- Implement security best practices for AI systems
- Understand data privacy requirements
- Apply governance frameworks for AI
- Ensure compliance with regulations

**Key Topics:**
- Data protection and privacy
- Model security and adversarial attacks
- Access control for AI services
- Audit trails and logging
- Regulatory compliance (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

## ðŸŽ¯ Study Tips

### Focus Areas
1. **Understand the "why"**: Focus on use cases and business value
2. **Learn service capabilities**: Know what each AWS AI service does
3. **Practice with Bedrock**: Hands-on experience with foundation models
4. **Study responsible AI**: Ethics and governance are heavily weighted
5. **Review real scenarios**: Understand practical application of concepts

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Don't memorize technical details - focus on concepts
- Don't ignore responsible AI topics - they're 28% of the exam
- Don't skip hands-on practice with AWS AI services
- Don't focus only on technical aspects - business context matters
- Don't underestimate the importance of security and compliance
